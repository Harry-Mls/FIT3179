Pokies in Victoria: What Communities are Impacted the Most?

Electronic Gaming Machines (EGMs), known as slot machines globally and "pokies" in Australia, have been a prominent part of the nationâ€™s culture since their legalisation in New South Wales in 1956. Originally introduced for entertainment in clubs and pubs, their popularity quickly spread nationwide. Today, Australia has one of the highest concentrations of pokies per capita. They are embedded in community venues such as RSLs and sports clubs, often marketed as sources of local funding. However, growing concerns around gambling addiction and financial harm have led to calls for reform. This report investigates which Local Government Areas (LGAs) are most heavily impacted, and the socio-economic conditions of these communities as indicated by the SEIFA DIS score (Socio-Economic Index).
